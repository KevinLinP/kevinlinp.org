  • Lightbox

    To enable very rapid development of generative Arduino LED lighting routines, I wrote a Meteor app that watches a local C file and compiles it to asm.js. The browser front-end then runs the asm.js file, and reads the output out of the asm.js 'memory space'. It then sends the output to D3.js, which renders it, allowing near-instant previews of the lighting routines.
